ABOUT US:-

Al-Noor Public School was founded in 1993, by Maulana Abdul Mobeen Asari,A determined Educationist and social worker.He focused on value education in islamic environment.
Al-Noor was stablished in Siswa - a Known village - in East Champaran Bihar, in 1993. And Al-noor Boy's Hostel was started in 2006.

AL-noor has achived various goals in this period.A lot of students passed out and have reached their favourite destinations .Many Doctors,Engineers, Other professionals,ullama,Huffaz and persons in other field in siswa and around, were nurtured and got their strong foundation in this school.

OUR MOSQUE


Here Hostel boys is read the salat and reading in Al noor masjid. Villages peoples are also read the salat with hostel boys.
A mosque that serves as a hostel for children who read the Salat is a wonderful place for young Muslims to live and grow in their faith. The hostel is typically located near a mosque or within a mosque complex, which allows the children to easily attend daily prayers and other religious activities. The hostel is equipped with facilities that are necessary for the children's comfort and safety, including dormitories, kitchens, and common areas for studying and socializing. The mosque hostel is also staffed with caretakers and religious leaders who provide guidance and support to the children. Overall, a mosque hostel is a valuable resource for families who want to ensure that their children have a strong Islamic upbringing.
